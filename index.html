FAQ

Am I truly anonymous when sending a message?
Technically yes. But also depends on your goal.
Can anyone see the message I send?
The developer can read your message.
How does my message work?
Your message will be placed manually in a self-destructing box. The countdown depends on the message length.
Can the recipient identify me?
It depends on your goal, but you can leave a clue if you want.
Can the recipient reply?
Yes, but their reply usually goes through the developer first. You will receive their reply via the self-destructing system. But only if you provide your account link.
Is the message permanently deleted?
Not 100%, but after it’s opened on each device it's gone. The developer is still working to make it fully privacy-proof.
How long does it take to deliver?
Usually 1 minute to process and about 30 seconds to send.

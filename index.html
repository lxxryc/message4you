FAQ

  • Am I truly anonymous when sending a message?

    Technically yes. But it also depends on your goal.

  • Can anyone see the message I send?

    The developer can read your message.

  • How does my message work?

    Your message will be placed manually in a self-destructing box. The countdown depends on the message length.

  • Can the recipient identify me?

    It depends on your goal, but you can leave a clue if you want.

  • Can the recipient reply?

    Yes, but their reply usually goes through the developer first. You will receive it via the self-destructing system if you provide your account link.

FAQ

Q: Am I truly anonymous when sending a message?

A: Yes, your identity is fully protected.

Q: Can anyone see the message I send?

A: No, it’s completely private.

Q: How do I send a message?

A: Your message will be placed in a self-destructing box. The countdown depends on the message length.

Q: Can the recipient identify me?

A: No, but you can leave a clue if you want.

Q: Can the recipient reply?

A: Yes, but only through the developer. You will receive their reply via the self-destructing system.

Q: Is the message permanently deleted?

A: Yes, after it’s opened on each device. We are working to make it 100% privacy-proof.

Q: How long does it take to deliver?

A: Usually 1 minute to process and about 30 seconds to send.
